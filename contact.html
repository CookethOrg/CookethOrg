FAQS

What services does Cooketh Company offer?

+

We design and build mobile apps, web apps, landing pages, and branding solutions using tools like Flutter, React, Figma, and more. From UI/UX to full-stack development, we create custom, scalable products tailored to your needs.

How does your process work?

+

It starts with you sharing your vision via our intake form. We follow up with a meeting, brainstorm concepts using sketches and Figma, and then build, test, and launch your project with ongoing support.

What makes Cooketh Company different?

+

We're a small, agile team of college students with a passion for bold, user-focused design and development. Our hackathon roots keep us fast, creative, and dedicated to delivering unique, high-quality solutions.

How can I start a project with you?

+

Fill out our intake form on our website with your idea and goals and schedule a meet with us. We’ll reach out to discuss your project, timeline, and budget, ensuring a smooth collaboration from concept to launch.
