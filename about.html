THE STORY OF
COOKETH COMPANY

(aka how a failed hackathon turned into a full-blown studio) We're just a couple of college students who accidentally started this. It all began with a hackathon project called Cooketh Flow, a chaotic mix of design sprints, energy drinks, way too many Figma tabs and thousands of lines of code. We didn't win (shocker), but what we did realize was this: We work surprisingly well together. Instead of going back to our solo journeys, we said, “Hey… what if we actually built something together?” So we did. Now we're Cooketh Company, a no-fluff design & dev studio crafting everything from brands and landing pages to full-blown apps. We're small, fast, and allergic to boring.We may not have trophies (yet), but we've got taste, teamwork, and just enough delusion to build some seriously epic solutions. And we are pretty fun to be around.
